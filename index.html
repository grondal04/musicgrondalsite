About Me

I. Beginnings

Back in 2019, when I was still in middle school, I discovered that I love 'creating stuffs' when I learn programming languages and used libraries to make games. During that time, I listened to rap music to relax, some names are Blacka, B-Ray, Hustlang, The Weeknd, and Travis Scott.

II. Immersing in Music

In 2020, I wrote my first songs and shared them on Facebook. Honestly, some were pretty bad, but that same year, I got serious about figuring out how to make a song go viral. I was thrilled when one Facebook video hit over 10,000 interactions and thousands of comments. That success fueled my passion for making music, and I kept at it with growing confidence.

III. Getting Serious

By 2021, I was making music seriously and regularly, releasing songs every 2-3 weeks and experimenting with different genres. I joined a music team in TÃ¢y Ninh, where we all shared a common vision and passion. I learned a ton from my teammates and their support was invaluable.

In 2022, I taught myself audio production, wrote over 100 songs, released about 20 tracks, appeared in four music videos, dropped one album, and performed at two shows. My music received a total of 1 million listens across various platforms, with two songs gaining significant popularity. None of this would have been possible without the guidance and feedback from my support network.

IV. Taking a Break

At the start of 2023, I began my first year as an IT major and found the workload overwhelming. From mid-2023 to 2024, I felt stuck because I didn't know how to capitalize on my popularity and monetize my music. So, I decided to take a break to gain more experience and explore business models. This period was challenging but incredibly insightful.
